Hi, I'm Lane. I build websites.

I'm a self taught front end developer who loves coding. Most of my jobs have been technology adjacent. I was selling, managing, or training people to use someone else's creations. This was satisfying for the most part, I enjoyed the interactions with my clients, translating tech to plain English, it was very rewarding seeing the lightbulbs go off above their heads, but something was missing. I was yearning to let my own creative juices flow, make my own whatever's that people would use and benefit from.

I pushed the urge to the back of my mind for as long as I could and then one day, I made a decision. Having no clue as to which direction I should take or how I could possibly get to where I wanted to go, I started searching. Finally, I took a step, and it felt great. I regained this almost childlike wonderment and curiosity. The more I learned, the more I found there was to learn. One more hill to ski, one more wave to ride, for every roadblock, there was a path that was just within reach. There's always a cool new tool to play with. Yeah, I was hooked.

I've had so many amazing mentors and teacher's along the way, Gordon Zhu, Joe from Coding Phase, Daniel from mmtuts, Erik Hanchett and Udemy courses among so many others. If you're interested in web development but need some direction, or a hand, or wonder what you got yourself into, shoot me an e-mail. The best thing that I have found is how welcoming, helpful, and encouraging the community can be and I am more than thrilled to be a part of it. Pay it forward...

You can find me on github here. Download my resume here, or connect with me here. Oh yeah, the dog that keeps popping up in my pictures is Neo, he's awesome.
